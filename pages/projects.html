FrontEnd Store

With this project, the simulation of an online t-shirt store was sought. It is a basic site but one that tests the use of technologies such as grid. The site has a responsive design that adapts it to mobile devices.

HTML5 languages were used to generate the structure and CSS3 for the page styles. In turn, it seeks to implement Javascript so that it generates interactions with the user, very soon.

Oftalmo Center

In OftalmoCenter we sought the simulation of a five-page institutional website, with the relevant information that this entails. From the beginning of this project it was proposed to increase the degree of difficulty with respect to its functionalities; therefore, in addition to the use of HTML5 and CSS3 is generated with Sass and Bootstrap5, so it has greater features that can provide a better user experience (UI).

Branding

Museo Barrilete

The next work was carried out in Córdoba, Argentina. A logo was redesigned for a museum called "Barrilete", this redesign had to meet the condition of identifying and representing children. In turn, it was observed that the original logo had too much information, so it was sought to make it cleaner, freeing it from certain elements.

For this challenge, different forms of barrels were studied and the structure of one was taken as a reference. Through its structure and composition (and decomposition) of elements, the different pieces were formed. graphs with the concept of rotation mainly. As for the color, cheerful colors that identified the children were sought.

Panadería Palmeras

The next work was carried out in Lima, Peru. A modern logo was designed for a bakery which sought to attract young audiences. At the time of doing this work, the bakery only had the name and a main color, so they were taken as a reference at the time of starting the work.

This challenge was worked mainly under the concept of repetition, rotation and pregnancy for position the brand in the mind of the consumer. In turn, two secondary colors were added to the graphic system, in addition to the primary one with the intention of give more variety to the pieces and the brand.

The presentation to the client was made through the following mockups so the brand already has a visual identity that can be recognized and remembered.
